Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify my student status?

You can verify your student status by uploading a valid student ID or an official enrollment letter from your institution. Once verified, you'll receive the student discount automatically applied to your account.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, the new rate will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

How does the 7-day free trial work?

You'll get full access to all features of your selected plan for 7 days without being charged. You can cancel anytime during the trial period and won't be billed. If you decide to continue, your subscription will automatically start after the trial ends.

What subjects are covered in the study guides?

Our study guides cover a wide range of subjects including Mathematics, Sciences (Biology, Chemistry, Physics), Computer Science, Engineering, Business, Economics, Arts, Humanities, and more. We're constantly adding new subjects based on student requests.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll still have access to your plan until the end of your current billing period.
